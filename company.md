# About Canvas & Co

Canvas & Co is a small art-print studio in Portland, Oregon. We design original
artwork in-house and print it on premium canvas, then ship it ready to hang. Our
work falls into three collections:

- **Landscape** — calm, golden-hour scenes: meadows, coastlines, mountains.
- **Botanical** — leaves, ferns, and plant studies in soft, natural tones.
- **Abstract** — color-field and geometric pieces for a modern wall.

We're a two-person studio. Most of our orders come from people furnishing a new
home or looking for a thoughtful gift, and a lot of our growth is word of mouth —
so showing people what's popular matters to us.

**Voice:** warm, human, handmade. We talk about art for people's homes, not
"products" or "solutions." Plain, friendly, unhurried.
