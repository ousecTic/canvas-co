# Design Guidelines

These apply to all **customer-facing** work (e.g. [index.html](index.html)). They do
**not** apply to the internal [sales-report.html](sales-report.html), which is a
private dashboard with its own clean dark-header style. Keep the two visual
languages distinct.

## Voice

Warm, human, handmade. Plain and friendly, never corporate or salesy — we make art
for people's homes. Talk about "art for people's homes," never "products" or
"solutions."

## Look & feel

Clean and calm with lots of white space, so the artwork stands out. Simple type,
big calm headings, rounded cards.

| Token | Value | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Background | `#fbfaf7` | Warm cream page background |
| Accent | `#b5673f` | Terracotta — links, accents, eyebrow labels |
