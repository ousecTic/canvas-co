A little about us

Canvas & Co is just the two of us, making original artwork by hand and printing it close to home. Most of our prints go to people settling into a new place or searching for a thoughtful gift — and most of them find us through someone they know.

Our work falls into three collections: Landscape, for calm golden-hour scenes; Botanical, for leaves and plant studies in soft natural tones; and Abstract, for color and shape on a modern wall.
